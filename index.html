Lets begin!
I (me/ben) was born on August 10, 1993 in Kitchener Ontario, Canada.
I was raised on a pig farm in Winterbourn and Fergus Ontario, Canada.
I moved to Waterloo Ontario, Canada in high school and attended Bluevale Collegiate Institute. Around this time I developed an appreciation for graffiti and illustration
After high school I moved to Banff Alberta, Canada with two of my best buds.
After the winter sneez’en I moved back to Ontario eventually settling down in Beaver Valley.
After moving back to my home province I started a new job at Beaver Valley Orchard and Cidery where I worked as the
cider makers assistantPicking apples, production maintanence
for 6 (happy) years…
and continued to work their for six years.
in 2019 i decided to enroll to the haliburton school of art + design.
before going to school i bought my first macbook.
i also bought a small house in owen sound.
